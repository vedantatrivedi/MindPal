What is MindPal?

Doctors, Mental Health Professionals, and Psychologists recommend the value of journaling that is keeping a diary, as a form of self therapy.

MindPal uses the power of Artificial Intelligence to improve your journaling experience, allowing you to visualise your emotional score and help you improve your mental health.
